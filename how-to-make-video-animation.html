How To Make Video Animation | 4 Ways

Video animations are an exquisite medium to communicate concepts narrate stories and capture the attention of viewers. Whether your goal is to produce animations for educational objectives, promotional endeavors, or individual undertakings. This guide will lead you through the steps of creating captivating video animations that leave a profound impression.

There are several ways you can create a video animation, depending on the type of video you want and how you want it.


Types Of Animation Video

  • 3D Video Animation
  • 2D Video Animation
  • Animation Explainer
  • Whiteboard Animation Explainer and so on

    • What is 3D animation

    3D animation is a process wherein three-dimensional digital environments, objects, and characters are created to generate dynamic and lifelike visuals. It utilizes computer-generated graphics to simulate depth, textures, lighting, and realistic movement. This particular form of animation provides a high level of visual realism and finds common applications in movies, video games, architectural visualization, product demos, and various other industries.

    What is 2D animation

    2D animation refers to the creation of animated visuals using two-dimensional graphics. It involves manipulating flat images or illustrations to create the illusion of movement through a series of frames. 2D animation techniques include traditional hand-drawn animation, digital vector-based animation, and motion graphics. 2D animation is widely used in cartoons, advertisements, explainer videos, educational content, and other forms of visual storytelling.

    What is Whiteboard Animation Explainer

    Whiteboard Animation Explainer is a specific style of video animation that simulates the process of drawing on a whiteboard. It typically involves an illustrated hand drawing and erasing images or text on a white background, accompanied by voiceover narration. Whiteboard animation is often used for educational videos, tutorials, presentations, and storytelling, as it can effectively communicate complex concepts in a simple and engaging manner.

    What is Animation Explainer

    Whiteboard explainer videos are concise and engaging animated videos that aim to explain a product, service, concept, or process. They combine visual elements, motion graphics, text, and voiceover narration to simplify complex information and deliver it in a clear and easily understandable manner. Animation explainer videos are commonly used for marketing, product demonstrations, training materials, and educational content, as they effectively communicate messages and capture audience attention. It can be 2D or 3D depending on the tool or software you used to create it.

    How To Create 3D Video Animation

    You can create 3D video animation using the following tool for both online and offline.

  • Renderforest
  • Plotagon
  • Powtoon
  • Blender

    • Renderforest

    Renderforest is an online tool where you can make all types of video animation including 3D, 2D, intro Video, whiteboard animation, and even logo design.

    Plotagon

    Plotagon is an Android app available on Google Play Store. You can create all kinds of 3D video animation, choose different available scenes, character and edit them.

    Powtoon

    Powtoon is also an online tool like Renderforest. You can make 3D animated videos with ease.

    Blender

    Blender is an advanced software that requires a blender skill to use. You can, however, create custom and advanced video animation.
